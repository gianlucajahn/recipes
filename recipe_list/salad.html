  6. Add salt and pepper and 1/4 cup of lemon juice.
  7. Whisk in half of the Parmesan cheese.
  8. Using your hands, tear off chunks of lettuce from the heads of romaine lettuce.
  9. Add to the dressing and toss until coated. Add the rest of the Parmesan cheese, and toss.
  10. Coarsely chop the toasted bread into croutons and add to the salad. Brush in any crumbs from chopping the bread, too.
  11. Serve immediately.
